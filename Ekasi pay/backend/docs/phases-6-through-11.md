# Roadmap phases 6–11 (external / product)

Engineering can prepare adapters and docs only. Activation requires evidence.

| Phase | Focus | Gate |
|---|---|---|
| 6 | Managed secrets, Redis, KYC storage, malware, sanctions, audit, monitoring, pen-test | Live credentials + remediations |
| 7 | Backups, PITR, restore RTO/RPO, load tests, IR/fraud playbooks, on-call | Drill artifacts |
| 8 | POPIA, FICA, product classification, disputes, partner certification | Legal sign-off; flags stay off |
| 9 | Frontend workspaces, offline, POS, inventory, onboarding, languages, support | Pilot readiness |
| 10 | Kimberley trader research → 5–10 then 50–100 merchants | Non-custodial features only |
| 11 | Single payment route, settlement, legal/accounting/security approval | Strict limits + clean reconcile |

Do not enable custodial money movement until Phases 1–8 gates for that product pass.

No row represents legal or regulatory approval. `External decision required` must be resolved by accountable counsel/provider owners before a production flag is enabled.

| Product | Production default | Proposed owner | Classification / decision |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Merchant ledger, sales, transfers, Cash Send | Posting disabled | Finance & Risk (TBD) | Payments, safeguarding, settlement and licensing analysis required |
| Lending | Disbursement disabled | Credit owner (TBD) | Credit-provider, affordability, disclosures and collections decisions required |
| Insurance | Activation/claim processing disabled | Insurance owner (TBD) | Intermediary/underwriter authority and claims/payout model required |
| Stokvel | Custodial movement disabled | Product & Compliance (TBD) | Custody, collective-scheme and mandate analysis required |
| Utilities | Live purchase disabled | Provider operations (TBD) | Provider contract, settlement, reversals and consumer-remedy decisions required |

Owners must record decision date, approver, evidence link, operating jurisdictions, limits, and renewal/review date before release.
